Facilities at Sri Mulam Club

Sri Mulam Club offers a curated blend of heritage, hospitality, and modern amenities. Designed to cater to members, guests, and prestigious events, our facilities ensure comfort, entertainment, and elegance in every experience. Explore the wide range of offerings within our historic premises.

Experience classic comfort and royal charm at the Sri Mulam Club Guest House. Designed to offer a peaceful retreat in the heart of Thiruvananthapuram, our guest rooms blend heritage ambiance with modern conveniences. Whether you're visiting for a special event or a leisurely stay, our accommodations ensure a warm and welcoming experience.

  • ❄️ Air Conditioning

  • 🛎️ Room Service
